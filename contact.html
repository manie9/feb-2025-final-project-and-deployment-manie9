Frequently Asked Questions

How do you verify the authenticity of UFO reports?

We use a multi-faceted approach to verify reports, including cross-referencing with other witnesses, checking for corroborating evidence (photos, videos, radar data), and consulting with experts in relevant fields. We also consider the credibility of witnesses and look for patterns across multiple reports.

Can I remain anonymous if I share my experience?

Absolutely. We respect your privacy and understand that many witnesses prefer to remain anonymous due to the sensitive nature of these experiences. We can use pseudonyms or omit identifying details while still sharing the important aspects of your encounter.

Do you investigate reports in person?

For significant cases that meet certain criteria, we do conduct in-person investigations when possible. This typically involves visiting the location, interviewing witnesses, and gathering any physical evidence that might be available. However, due to resource limitations, we cannot investigate every report in person.

How can I support your research?

The best ways to support our work are by sharing your own experiences, helping to spread awareness about our blog, and engaging with our content through comments and social media. If you have specialized expertise (e.g., in aviation, astronomy, or photography), we also welcome your insights on specific cases.
