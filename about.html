About The Blog

Our mission and purpose in exploring the unknown

Our Mission

MANIE'S BLOG OF CURIOSITY, MYSTERY & HISTORY was founded with a simple yet profound mission: to explore and document the unexplained phenomena that challenge our understanding of reality.

We believe that by examining UFO sightings, extraterrestrial encounters, and other mysterious events with an open mind and critical thinking, we can expand our knowledge of the universe and our place within it.

Our approach combines thorough research, witness testimonies, and historical context to provide a comprehensive view of each case we investigate. We strive to present information in an accessible and engaging way, making complex topics understandable to readers of all backgrounds.

Our Values

  • Curiosity: We embrace the unknown with an insatiable desire to learn and understand.
  • Objectivity: We examine evidence without preconceived notions or biases.
  • Respect: We treat witnesses and their experiences with dignity and compassion.
  • Integrity: We commit to honest reporting and transparent research methods.
  • Community: We foster an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives are welcomed.

About the Author

MANIE

MANIEVhas been fascinated by UFO phenomena and extraterrestrial encounters since witnessing an unexplained aerial object as a teenager. With a background in journalism and astronomy, Mane brings a unique perspective to the investigation of mysterious events.

After graduating with a degree in Journalism from the University of Arizona, Mane spent several years working as an investigative reporter before dedicating full-time to researching and documenting UFO cases.

MANIE has interviewed hundreds of witnesses, attended numerous conferences on extraterrestrial phenomena, and traveled to UFO hotspots around the world. This blog represents the culmination of over a decade of research and investigation into the unknown.

"I don't claim to have all the answers. In fact, I believe that embracing the mystery and asking better questions is more important than arriving at definitive conclusions. My goal is to document these phenomena with integrity and to create a space where curious minds can explore the possibilities together."
